Invention #8 - Returning Weapon

Sometimes I feel like I should be doing my artificer-y things. Y'know, so maybe when I get out there, I'll look more of the part. Apparently this is a somewhat common project for beginners, but I think that I'll try to... put my own spin on it?

The way this one works is that you're meant to enchant the handle of the weapon to recognise that it was thrown to begin with and where to return to. A secondary enchantment also goes on the blade, axehead, etc, to indicate a hit - this emits a signal back to the handle to return.

I decided to enchant a handaxe that was lying around the house (don't judge me). It's a little bit finicky and difficult to explain, but I recommened carving the glyphs into the material if you can.

It does work as expected! Just don't try to chop down a tree with it.
